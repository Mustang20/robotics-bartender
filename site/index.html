THE CONCEPT

A robotic arm that serves — you supply the drinks, we supply the spectacle.

We call the system Robobar — a precision robotic arm built to be the centerpiece of the room, not just the bar.

01

Champagne, wine, spirits or mocktails

The arm handles bottles, pours into flutes, glasses or shot glasses, and repeats the exact same motion every time — whatever you're serving, the pour is identical, glass after glass.

02

Battery or mains — goes anywhere

Robobar runs on battery for full off-grid freedom, or plugs into a standard 230V socket when one's nearby. Set it up on the lawn, the rooftop or the show floor — wherever the event actually is.

03

Built-in marketing tools

Gate each pour behind a QR code, a short survey or a follow on your event's social account — guests get a drink, you get a lead or a new follower.
